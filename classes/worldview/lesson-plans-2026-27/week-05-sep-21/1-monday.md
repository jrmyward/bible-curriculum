## Why This Day Is the Sub Day (rather than the original Day 8 Four-Test)

Mon Sep 21 was originally scheduled as Day 8 = Four-Test self-application. But the Four-Test day is the **model exercise** of the year — *"every later unit's Day 7 follows this template"* (per the teaching map). Delivering the model exercise via a sub would weaken year-1 calibration of the exercise the rest of the year copies.

Resolution: **Day 8 and Day 10 were swapped.** Today (Sep 21, sub) is now Contemporary Resource — a video + structured debrief day that runs cleanly with a sub. Wed Sep 23 is now Day 10 = Four-Test (you teach it).

Pedagogical bonus: students hear a thoughtful contemporary Christian voice (Keller / Collins / McLaughlin) **before** applying the four tests to Christianity themselves on Day 10.
