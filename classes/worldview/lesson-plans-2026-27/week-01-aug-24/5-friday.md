## Teacher Notes

- Day 5 is an important day despite the absence. The reflection journal is the first graded artifact of the year. Set the sub up for success and students will take the assignment seriously.
- When journals are returned Monday, read 1–2 strong ones aloud (with permission). This models the voice we want students to develop.
- If a student didn't complete the journal (some will blow it off because it's a sub day), have a brief private conversation Monday. Don't make it punitive — make it formative. This is your first chance to set the expectation that sub-day work counts.
