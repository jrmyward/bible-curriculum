# Worldview — Teaching Map

**School Year:** 2026–2027  
**Grade Level:** 11–12  
**Textbook:** *Understanding the Times: A Survey of Competing Worldviews* by Jeff Myers  
**Supplemental Coverage:** Buddhism, Hinduism, Mormonism (LDS)  
**Total Instructional Days:** 162 days across 35 weeks  
**Trimester Breakdown:** T1: 52 days | T2: 58 days | T3: 52 days

---

## Unit Overview

| Unit | Weeks | Worldview(s) | Type | Key Question | Capstone |
|------|-------|--------------|------|--------------|----------|
| **1** | 1–3 | Christianity (Biblical Theism) | Core | What is the biblical worldview across all disciplines? | No |
| **2** | 4–5 | Mormonism (LDS) | Supplemental | Is Mormonism Christian, or a distinct religion? | No |
| **3** | 6–8 | Islam | Core | How does Islamic theology shape law, politics, and culture? | Yes (T1) |
| **4** | 9–11 | Buddhism & Hinduism | Supplemental | How do Eastern religions differ from Western monotheism? | No |
| **5** | 12–14 | Secularism (Naturalism) | Core | Can secularism provide a coherent foundation for ethics and meaning? | Yes (T2) |
| **6** | 15–17 | Marxism | Core | What are the failures of Marxist ideology in theory and practice? | No |
| **7** | 18–20 | Postmodernism | Core | How does postmodernism deconstruct truth and morality? | Yes (T2/T3) |
| **8** | 21–24 | Cross-Worldview Synthesis | Application | How do all 8 worldviews answer life's ultimate questions? | No |
| **9** | 25–28 | Contemporary Issues | Application | How does worldview shape responses to bioethics, justice, and technology? | No |
| **10** | 29–33 | Final Capstones & Exam | Assessment | How has this course shaped your worldview? | Yes (Final) |
