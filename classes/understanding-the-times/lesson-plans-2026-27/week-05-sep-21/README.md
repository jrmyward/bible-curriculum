## Week Overview

This is the **dense week** of Unit 1. Five teaching days, four cohort tools (Contemporary Resource, lecture, Four-Test small-group, Gospel-Conversation roleplay, independent writing), one sub day. The week ends Friday with the Worldview Brief — students' first major individual artifact of the year.

### Daily Plans

- **Mon (Sep 21)** · Day 8 · **SUB DAY** — Contemporary Resource (video + structured debrief)
- **Tue (Sep 22)** · Day 9 · The Christian Family (Catholic · Orthodox · Protestant survey)
- **Wed (Sep 23)** · Day 10 · Four-Test Self-Application (the **model exercise** for the year)
- **Thu (Sep 24)** · Day 11 · Gospel Conversation roleplay (the **foundational exercise** the rest of the year copies)
- **Fri (Sep 25)** · Day 12 · Worldview Brief writing (independent, in-class)

### About the Sep 21 Sub Day

Mon Sep 21 is a planned teacher absence. The Unit 1 day-by-day was **rearranged** to put the Contemporary Resource day on Sep 21 (originally Day 10) and move the Four-Test self-application to Wed Sep 23 (originally Day 8) — so that the model-exercise day lands on a teacher day. See [teaching-map.md](../../teaching-map.md) § Unit 1 Day 8 and Day 10 entries for the swap rationale, and [substitute-plan-monday.md](substitute-plan-monday.md) for the full sub plan.

### Assessments This Week

- Day 8 (Mon, sub): 3×5 cards from each student with their *"thing that struck me"* + their *"one question."* Sub collects.
- Day 9 (Tue): nothing collected.
- Day 10 (Wed): small-group Four-Test worksheets (one per group). Graded for completion + both halves engaged (passes AND hard questions).
- Day 11 (Thu): 3×5 card from each student with *"one sentence about what you learned today."*
- Day 12 (Fri): the **Worldview Brief** (2–3 handwritten pages or 600–900 typed words). Graded for completion + accuracy + own-voice.

### Materials Needed

- Day 8 (sub): video queued, 3×5 cards, sub folder with attendance, seating, plan
- Day 9: Nicene Creed handout (or reference §Part 3 Source 2 of reading packet)
- Day 10: printed Four-Test small-group worksheet (one per group of 3–4)
- Day 11: printed Skeptic roleplay cards (4 cards — Sarah, Marcus, Priya, Daniel — in facilitation guide §Day 11)
- Day 12: printed Worldview Brief assignment (one per student); independent work, no group materials

### Sub Day Prep (Friday Sep 18 = Teacher Work Day — use it for this)

- Queue the Contemporary Resource video (Tim Keller / Francis Collins / Rebecca McLaughlin — your call). See [Unit 1 lecture companion §Day 8 — Three suggested resources](../../lectures/unit-01-christianity-lecture-companion.md).
- Print enough 3×5 cards (or 1/4 sheets of paper) for the class + 5 extras.
- Stage the sub folder on your desk with: attendance roster, seating chart, the full sub plan ([substitute-plan-monday.md](substitute-plan-monday.md)), video link/login, collection envelope labeled *"Day 8 Cards — Mon Sep 21."*

### Notes

- **Day 10 is the highest-leverage day of Unit 1.** The Four-Test self-application is the *model exercise* — every later worldview unit's Day 7 follows this template. Take it seriously.
- **Day 11 is the second highest-leverage day.** The Gospel Conversation is the *foundational exercise* the rest of the year copies. Get the structure right; the rest of the year adapts it.
- **Friday's Worldview Brief is yours, not the sub's** — it requires the teacher of record to circulate and answer per-student questions during the writing block.
