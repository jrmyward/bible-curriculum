## How to Use This Outline as You Study

1. After Day 1: Read the framing and the four claims about worldviews.
2. During Days 2–4: Fill in Sections 1, 2, 3, and 5 as each concept is taught.
3. Before Day 6 (Steel-Manning practice): Re-read Section 6. Pick a view you disagree with and practice steel-manning it in your head before class.
4. Before Day 7 (Worldview Grid): Fill in Section 4.
5. Before Day 8 (Mini Pair & Defend): Re-read the standing rule in Section 6.
6. Before Day 10 (Doug Letter due + Test): Sketch your Doug response. Quiz yourself on every term in the *Publisher Vocabulary* section of the reading packet.
