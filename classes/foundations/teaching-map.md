## Design Philosophy

**Compress the publisher's pacing; layer lightly on top of it.** Summit designs Foundations as a 180-day daily-lesson course (18 chapters × 10 lessons, with a Chapter Test on Lesson 10). Our usable 2026–27 calendar is **145 instructional days**, so each chapter is compressed to **~8 days** by merging the publisher's thinner lessons — the spine, the sequence, and the chapter tests are kept intact.

**Freshman-appropriate delivery.** This is a lecture-seminar course, not the cohort/debate model used in the 11–12 classes. Ninth graders meeting this material for the first time are best served by teacher-led content with guided notes, punctuated by discussion and application, and anchored by the publisher's chapter tests and student Study Guide. We generate only what the book doesn't provide (pacing, lesson-sequencing plans, application handouts) and reuse what it does (tests, discussion questions with answers, Study Guide, key terms/verses).

**Trimester seams fall on the book's own seams.** Each trimester closes on a chapter test, and each break lands between chapters rather than inside one:

- **T1 ends after Act 1: Creation** (Ch 6 test, Nov 10).
- **T2 ends exactly on the story's climax** (Ch 12, New Creation — test on the T2 end date, Feb 25).
- **T3 ends after the Conclusion** (Ch 18 test, May 14), leaving the final light week for course wrap.

## Class Periods

Periods run **~60 minutes most days**, **~45 minutes on Wednesdays** (a shorter schedule).
Lesson plans time each day accordingly — Wednesdays get a tighter plan (good for tests,
review, or a single focused activity rather than content + activity).

## Per-Chapter Rhythm (8-day block)

| Block day | Activity |
|---|---|
| 1–5 | **Lecture + guided notes** — the chapter's content lessons, merged where the publisher splits thin ones |
| 6 | **Seminar / application** — teacher-manual discussion questions (provided with answers); connect to life |
| 7 | **Review** — key terms & key verses (publisher's "Lesson 9"); student-manual Study Guide |
| 8 | **Chapter Test** — publisher `TR <ch>.10A` |

Longer blocks (9 days) add one lecture day. `**SUB:**` days are teacher absences (school in session) — run a self-contained activity a substitute can manage (chapter test, Study Guide work, film + worksheet, in-class writing). Detailed sub plans come from `/generate-substitute-plan`.

## Assessment Summary

- **18 chapter tests**, publisher-provided (`TR <ch>.10A`), one on each chapter's final block day. Reused as-is.
- **Student Study Guide** (publisher, in the student manual) is the standing pre-test review tool.
- **Trimester grades** rest on the publisher chapter tests (T1→Ch 6, T2→Ch 12, T3→Ch 18) plus in-class discussion and application work.
