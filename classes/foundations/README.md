## Status

**Being built.** Vol. 1 of Summit's _Understanding the Times_ high-school series (18
chapters). All 18 chapters of the textbook + teacher manual are transcribed. Scope,
pacing, and delivery model are decided (see below); lesson plans are the next build.

**Decisions locked in:**

- **Full-year course**, compressed from the publisher's 180-day / 10-lessons-per-chapter
  design to **~8 days per chapter** across the 145 usable 2026–27 days.
- **Lecture-seminar delivery** (teacher-led content + discussion/application), *not* the
  cohort/debate model of the 11–12 classes (not for freshmen new to the material).
- **Lean layer on the publisher**: reuse the provided chapter tests, discussion questions
  (with answers), and student Study Guide; generate only pacing, lesson-sequencing plans,
  and application handouts.
- **Three parts, aligned to trimesters**: Worldview & the Word (Ch 1–4) · The Story of
  the Bible / six-act metanarrative (Ch 5–12) · Knowing & Following God (Ch 13–18).
