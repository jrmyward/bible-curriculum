# Foundations — Lesson Plans 2026–27

Day-by-day lesson plans for the 18-chapter Foundations course, paced per the
[teaching map](../teaching-map.md).

## Approach

- **Compressed publisher pacing.** Each chapter's 10 publisher lessons are sequenced into
  the chapter's ~8-day block (see the teaching map) by *merging* thin lessons, not cutting
  activities.
- **Lean layer.** Plans sequence and time the publisher's own materials (activities from the
  teacher-resources PDF, discussion questions with answers from the teaching manual, chapter
  tests from `portal/chNN/`). They cite those sources rather than reproduce them, and add a
  new handout only where the manual is thin.
- **Period lengths.** ~60 min most days; **~45 min on Wednesdays** (short schedule). Each day's
  timed schedule reflects its actual length. Put tests/review/single-activity days on Wednesdays.
- **Activity rhythm.** Not every day is content delivery — the publisher builds in games,
  group activities, a video day, mapmaking, and review-games. Sub days use the self-contained
  activities (e.g., the Ch 1 video day lands on the Aug 28 sub day).

## Current format (interim)

One file per chapter: `chNN-lesson-plan.md`, containing all ~8 days of that chapter's block.
Easy to review and print as a chapter packet.
