## Teaching Outline (~60 min)

*Merges publisher Lessons 4 and 5 — the four short reflective previews.*

### 0:00–0:10 · Warm-up — Beliefs & Behavior Brainstorm (manual p.11)

- T-chart on the board: **Beliefs Affect Behavior** / **Behavior Affects Beliefs.**
- Seed each side (trust fall → beliefs shape action; gratitude journal → action reshapes beliefs), then students add examples. Sets up why knowing *and living* the faith both matter.

### 0:10–0:32 · Read & discuss §§1.7–1.10

- **Knowing God** (why *"the fear of the LORD is the beginning of wisdom,"* Prov 9:10) and **knowing ourselves** (identity as image-bearers).
- **Becoming like Christ** as a **lifelong process**, helped by spiritual disciplines (prayer, Bible reading, giving).
- **Love as the Christian's primary calling** — self-giving love, seeking others' good before our own.
- The conclusion's **four invitations**: locate yourself re: the faith; stay open; discuss with classmates; pray.
- Use manual Qs from **pp.10–13** — pick 5–6; favor 1.8 (Christlikeness) and 1.9 (love).

### 0:32–0:55 · Activity — Being a Christian (`TR 1.5A`, pairs; manual p.13)

- Pairs read quotations on what it means to be a Christian (believers *and* skeptics) and relate each to the **misconceptions / definition** from §1.2.
- Share out the ones that provoked debate. **Purpose:** sharpen the real definition against the caricatures.

### 0:55–0:60 · Homework + sub-day briefing

- Tell students **tomorrow is a sub day**: six short videos + questions, done independently — it counts like any class day.
