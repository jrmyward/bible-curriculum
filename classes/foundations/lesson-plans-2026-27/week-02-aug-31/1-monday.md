## Teaching Outline (~60 min)

### 0:00–0:10 · Set up (`PS 1.6A`; manual p.14)

- Display the slides. In groups of 4–5, build a map of the Christian worldview — either a **geographical map** (title, direction, legend, scale, labels) **or** a **mind map** (title, labels, showing relative importance and relationships of ideas).

### 0:10–0:38 · Build

- Groups brainstorm on scratch paper, then design their poster.
- **Circulate and push** them to include: the **six acts**, the **definition of a worldview**, **who Jesus is**, **why the Bible matters** — the chapter's big ideas become map features.

### 0:38–0:56 · Present

- Each group presents and explains its map. Class notes similarities and differences.

### 0:56–0:60 · Close

- *"You just drew the map for the whole year — every chapter fills in more of it."* Assign test study.
