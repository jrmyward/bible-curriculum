# Understanding the Faith — Official Syllabus

*Extracted from Understanding the Faith: A Survey of Christian Apologetics by Jeff Myers (Summit) on 2026-04-22*

---

## Course Overview

This course provides a comprehensive survey of Christian apologetics, equipping students to understand and defend the Christian faith. The curriculum is organized into 18 chapters covering foundational questions about truth, God, the Bible, Christianity, and ethical living.

## Pacing Notes

- **Total Duration**: 180 instructional days (36 weeks at 5 days per week)
- **Structure**: Each chapter includes reading assignments, discussion questions, activities, quizzes, and a chapter test
- **Assessment Pattern**: Chapters typically conclude with a test and assignment collection
- **Format**: Mix of in-class discussions, at-home reading, exploration activities, and video content
