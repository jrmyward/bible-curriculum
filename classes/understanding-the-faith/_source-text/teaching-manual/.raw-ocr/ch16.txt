"THE GOD DELUSION" DEBATE CLIP
This except from the debate between Dr. Richard Dawkins and Dr. John Lennox, both
of Oxford University, focuses on Dawkins's popular book The God Delusion and his as-
sertion that evolution offers a better explanation for creation than Christianity or other
religions. He believes these have been superseded by Darwin's discoveries. Dawkins
insists, "What religion once aspired to explain is now better explained in a more beauti-
ful and elegant fashion by science."
Lennox points out that science is limited; it cannot answer the basic questions of life:
Who am I? What is the purpose of my existence? Science requires faith as much as
Christianity does— not blind faith, but evidence-based faith, which the Christian finds
in science, history, and personal experience. The difference between science and religion
is more a matter of worldview than of evidence.
