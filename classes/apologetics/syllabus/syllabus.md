## Course Overview

Apologetics is the second volume in the four-part _Understanding the Times_ series, and uses
Scripture and a Student Textbook as primary texts to:

- help students understand, experience, and live out the Christian worldview;
- guide students in sharing, defending, and commending the Christian faith to others;
- demonstrate how the Christian worldview stands up to common objections in society today;
- equip students with biblical teaching on topics including faith, pluralism, relativism,
  science, evil, hell, violence, gender, and more.

The course integrates the content with a review of the Bible's metanarrative and a defense of
its trustworthiness, to prepare students to share the gospel with sensitivity and critical
thinking.

  them into ~8 class sessions (paired subsections + a video lesson + a study-guide/test day).
