# Apologetics — Lesson Plans 2026–27

Day-by-day lesson plans for the 18-chapter Apologetics course, paced per the
[teaching map](../teaching-map.md).

## Approach

- **Publisher model, compressed pacing.** Each chapter's 10 subsections are sequenced into
  the chapter's ~8-day block (see the teaching map) by *merging* thin subsections, not cutting
  activities. Delivery is teacher-led content + the publisher's built-in activities and
  discussion questions — the same model as Foundations.
- **Lean layer.** Plans sequence and time the publisher's own materials (activities from the
  teacher-resources PDF, discussion questions with answers from the teacher manual, videos, the
  Study Guide, and the `TR n.8` chapter tests from `portal/chNN/`). They cite those sources
  rather than reproduce them, and add a new handout only where the manual is thin.
- **Signature debate days (surgical).** On five back-half objection chapters — **Ch 8, 9, 11,
  13, 14** — the chapter's seminar/application day is run as a *defense-first* Pair & Defend or
  Case Study (students commit to / defend a position before the answer is taught). See the
  [teaching map](../teaching-map.md#signature-debate-days-inventory) and
  [`_shared/cohort-tools.md`](../../../_shared/cohort-tools.md). These are optional/adjustable;
  everything else stays publisher content-first.
- **Period lengths.** ~60 min most days; **~45 min on Wednesdays** (short schedule). Each day's
  timed schedule reflects its actual length. Put tests/review/single-activity days on Wednesdays.
- **Activity rhythm.** Not every day is content delivery — the publisher builds in games, group
  activities, a video day, Bible studies, and review. Sub days use the self-contained activities
  (see the sensitive-topic caution in the teaching map for the April sub cluster on Ch 15–16).

## Format

**Week style.** One folder per calendar week (`week-NN-mon-DD/`) with a file per day
(`1-monday.md` … `5-friday.md`), a `substitute-plan-*.md` on sub days, a `README.md`, and a
`handouts/` folder. **Week folders are created per chapter by `/build-chapter apologetics N`** —
they are not pre-stamped. An 8-day chapter spans ~2 calendar-week folders and can start
mid-week; a chapter boundary inside a week leaves the rest of that week as stubs for the next
chapter. No-school days get a short marker file, not a lesson. See
[`_shared/lesson-plan-standards.md`](../../../_shared/lesson-plan-standards.md) for the exact
publisher day-file template (Start Slide · Teaching Outline · Slide-Deck Outline · …) — the
**Foundations Ch 1 exemplar** (`classes/foundations/lesson-plans-2026-27/week-01-aug-24/`) is
the model to match.

## Shared directories

- `handouts/` — handouts reused across multiple weeks (week-specific handouts live in each
  `week-NN/handouts/`).
- `assessments/` — publisher model: the per-chapter assessment is the publisher `TR n.8` test
  (built as a Google Form quiz by `/publish-chapter`), so no shared cohort rubrics here. The
  signature-debate-day chapters may add a Pair & Defend / Case Study rubric when built.
