## Teaching Outline (~60 min)

### 0:00–0:06 · Do Now & attendance
- Students define the four key terms cold; go over quickly so misdefinitions surface now, not on the test.

### 0:06–0:30 · Activity — *Venturing into Evangelism* (TR 1.7; pairs; ~20 min)
- Read **Matthew 4:19** to the class. In pairs, work the **Rough Seas** diagram: brainstorm causes of resistance under **Emotions · Reason · Relationships · Desires/Loves.** Then the **Calm Seas** diagram (receptiveness).
- Discuss: *"How can these four influences help you engage more graciously next time you talk with a non-Christian?"*

### 0:30–0:48 · Chapter 1 Review (PS 1.7)
- Walk the review deck. Hit the test's heavy items (see Teacher Notes): the **four key verses** (esp. Isaiah 1:18, Matt 28:18–20), the **mission of God** (bless/redeem/restore), the **Great Commission's three lessons**, **evangelism's three parts**, and the **apologetics definition** (defending/commending/translating).
