**Apologetics · Bible 10/11 · Chapter 1, Day 5 (video day)**

## Video 1 — What Is Apologetics, and Why Does It Matter? *(Mittelberg)*

1. From what Scripture does the concept of apologetics come?
2. What does Peter tell us about apologetics?

## Video 2 — What Kind of Questions Does Apologetics Answer? *(Sharp)*

1. What are the two parts of apologetics?
2. What does it mean to "play offense" in apologetics?
3. What does it mean to "play defense" in apologetics?

## Video 3 — Can Christians Be Effective Apologists without Being Experts? *(McDowell)*

1. Who is called to do apologetics?
2. Explain the benefits of saying, "I don't know. Let me get back to you."
3. The speaker advised taking one step to "start training your ______ and getting in the game."

## Video 4 — Isn't Faith Blind? *(McDowell)*

1. Explain the problem with saying, "Just have faith."
2. What is a common, but mistaken, definition for the word *faith*?
3. According to the speaker, what is one reason Jesus performed miracles and rose from the dead?
4. According to the speaker, what is faith?

## Video 5 — What Is the Role of Apologetics in Christianity? *(Wallace)*

1. What do many people in the church feel about apologetics?
2. How did Jesus show that evidence matters?
3. Why was it so important for Jesus to emphasize evidence to his disciples?
4. How did Jesus respond when John the Baptist had doubts and sent his disciples to him?
5. Why is apologetics needed if God's Word has power and God calls whom he wills?
6. What does the speaker say about the relationship between evangelism and apologetics?
