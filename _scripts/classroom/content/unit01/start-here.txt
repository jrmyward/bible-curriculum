Welcome to Foundations. This first unit introduces the big idea behind the whole course: a worldview, the "map" you use to make sense of life, and what it actually means to follow Jesus.

Questions we'll wrestle with this unit:
• What is a worldview, and how does mine shape the way I live?
• What does it mean to be a Christian, and what does it not mean?
• Why should Christians trust the Bible and live under its authority?
• What story is the Bible telling, and where do I fit in it?
• How do our beliefs shape our actions, and our actions our beliefs?

By the end of this unit, you'll be able to:
• Define a worldview and give examples.
• Explain what it means to be a Christian, and clear up three common misconceptions.
• Say why Christians trust the Bible, even though it's an ancient book.
• Name two common mistakes people make when reading the Bible.
• Tell the Bible's story in six acts, from Creation to New Creation.
• Explain why love is a Christian's primary calling.
