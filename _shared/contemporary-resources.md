# Contemporary Resource Library

A curated master list of replacement and supplemental resources for use across Bible curriculum classes. These resources are designed to bring contemporary voices, current events, and modern examples into lessons built on the *Understanding the Times* framework.

Use these to replace outdated illustrations in the textbook, enrich discussion prompts, or provide students with material that connects course concepts to the world they actually live in.

---

## How to Use This List

- **Before each unit**, scan the relevant category below for resources that connect to the chapter's themes.
- **For discussion briefs**, consider assigning a short article or video as a companion to the reading.
- **For case studies**, use current events from these sources as scenario material.
- Resources marked with **(S)** are suitable to share directly with students. All others are teacher-reference only — adapt as needed.

## Adding Resources

When you find a new resource worth tracking, add it to the appropriate category above using the same table format. Include the **(S)** tag if it's appropriate to share directly with students. Prefer resources that are:

1. Currently available (in print, online, or streaming)
2. Accessible to a high school audience (or easily adapted)
3. Substantive — not just inspirational, but intellectually engaging
4. From a range of Christian traditions (not just one denominational perspective)
